# Third-party board and piece themes

JuChess includes optional visual themes collected from the projects below. The original authors retain all rights to their work. This notice describes the source of the imported files; it does not replace each project's license.

## Chess.com Boards and Pieces

- Source: <https://github.com/GiorgioMegrelli/chess.com-boards-and-pieces>
- Imported: board textures and the complete piece directories exposed by the repository.
- License status: the source repository did not include a license file when these assets were imported. Treat these files as unlicensed third-party material unless permission is obtained from the relevant rights holders.

## Kiwen Suwi

- Source: <https://github.com/neverRare/kiwen-suwi>
- Imported: the current Kiwen Suwi and Kiwen Suwi High Contrast PNG pieces, plus the High Contrast board.
- Artwork license: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International.
- Font license: SIL Open Font License 1.1. The font itself is not bundled in JuChess.

## Chessboard.js Themes

- Source: <https://github.com/jbkunst/chessboardjs-themes>
- Imported: the seven board color themes and eight complete PNG piece sets.
- Repository license: MIT. Some piece artwork is adapted from upstream chess projects and may retain its upstream terms; consult the source repository before commercial redistribution.

## Lichess

- Source: <https://github.com/lichess-org/lila>
- Imported: complete standard piece sets (SVG or WebP), 2D board textures, and Staunton 3D board textures.
- Excluded: the incomplete six-file `mono` directory, board-state overlays, thumbnails regenerated by JuChess, and original-resolution duplicates.
- License details: see Lichess's [`COPYING.md`](https://github.com/lichess-org/lila/blob/master/COPYING.md). Board textures and piece sets use different licenses, including AGPL, GPL, MIT, Creative Commons, non-commercial Creative Commons, and author-specific terms. The `public/images/board` collection is listed under AGPL-3.0-or-later and `public/images/staunton` under MIT.

## Distribution warning

Several imported themes have non-commercial, attribution, share-alike, author-specific, or unclear licensing. Before distributing JuChess commercially, review every selected theme in the source projects and remove or replace any asset for which the intended use is not permitted.
